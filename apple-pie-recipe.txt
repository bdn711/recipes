FRENCH APPLE PIE
Ingredients:
8-INCH
- Pastry for 8-inch two-crust pie
- 1/2 c. sugar
- 2 tbsp. flour (if using self-rising flour, omit salt)
- 1/4 tsp. ground nutmeg
- 1/4 tsp. ground cinnamon
- dash of salt
- 5 c. thinly sliced pared tart apples (about 5 medium)
- 1 tbsp. margarine or butter

9-inch
- Pastry for 9-inch Two-Crust Pie
- 3/4 c. sugar
- 1/4 c. all-purpose flour (if using self-rising flour, omit salt)
- 1/2 tsp. ground nutmeg
- 1/2 tsp. ground cinnamon
- dash of salt
- 6 c. thinly sliced pared tart apples (about 6 medium)
- 2 tbsp. margarine or butter

Directions:
1. Heat oven to 425 degrees F.
2. Prepare pastry (or use storebought pastry).
3. Mix sugar, flour, nutmeg, cinnamon, and salt.
4. Stir in apples.
5. Turn into pastry-lined pie plate; dot with margarine.
6. Cover with top crust that has slits cut in it; seal and flute.
7. Cover edge with 3-inch strip of aluminum foil; remove foil during last 15 minutes of baking.
8. Bake until crust is brown and juice begins to bubble through slits in crust, 40 to 50 minutes.

VARIATIONS:
Apple Cheese Pie:
Prepare 9-inch pie as directed except--pour half of the apple mixture into
pastry-lined pie plate; cover with 5 sliced process American cheese and top with
remaining apples.

Canned Apple Pie:
Prepare 9-inch pie as directed except--substitute 2 cans
(20 oz. each) pie sliced apples, drained, for the fresh apples.

Dutch Apple Pie:
Prepare 9-inch pie as directed except--make extra large slits in top crust; 5
minutes before end of baking, pour 1/2 cup whipping cream through slits in top
crust. Best served warm.

French Apple Pie:
Prepare pastry for 9-inch one-crust pie; omit margarine and top apple filling
with crumb topping; Mix 1 cup all-purpose flour, 1/2 cup firm margarine or
butter and 1/2 cup packed brown sugar until crumbly. Bake 50 minutes. Cover
topping with aluminum foil during last 10 minutes of baking. Best served warm.

Green Apple Pie:
Prepare pie as directed except--increase sugar 1/2 cup and use green apples.
