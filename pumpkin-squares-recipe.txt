PUMPKIN SQUARES
Ingredients:
Crust
- 1 package yellow cake mix less 1 cup
- 1 egg
- 1/3 c. melted butter

Filling
- 1 1/2 c. pumpkin
- 3 eggs
- 3/4 c. sugar
- 2/3 c. canned milk
- 1/2 tsp. cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp. pumpkin pie spice

Topping
- 1 c. cake mix
- 1 tsp. cinnamon
- 3 Tbsp. soft butter

Directions:
1. Mix crust ingredients together and press in bottom of greased 9x13 pan.
2. Mix and pour filling ingredients over crust.
3. Combine and sprinkle topping over mixture & bake at 325 degrees F for 45 minutes.
